## üìù Content Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- Conversational, helpful, friendly
- Expert but not condescending  
- Practical and actionable
- Include personal touches ("from my experience...")
- Use "you" and "your" frequently
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Break up text with subheadings, lists, tables

### Structure
Each cluster article should include:
1. Hook/intro addressing user intent
2. Quick answer (for featured snippets)
3. Detailed breakdown
4. Practical tips/examples
5. Related tools link
6. FAQ section (3-5 questions)
7. Link to pillar + related clusters

### Word Count Targets
- Pillar: 2,500-3,000 words
- Clusters: 1,200-1,800 words

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Every cluster links to pillar
- Pillar links to all clusters
- Related clusters interlink (e.g., Business Hours ‚Üî Stock Market)
- All articles link to relevant tools
